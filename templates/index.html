Understand what each area type means for your property
Super Built-up Area

Includes the built-up area plus proportionate share of common areas like lobby, staircase, lift, and amenities. Usually slightly higher price per sqft.

Built-up Area

Actual constructed area including internal walls but excluding common areas. Usually slightly lower than super built-up area in price per sqft.

Carpet Area

Usable area within the walls of your apartment. Excludes balcony, walls, and common areas. Price per sqft is usually highest because it's fully usable space.

Plot Area

Total land area of the property including open spaces. Useful for villas or independent houses. Price per sqft depends on land value.
